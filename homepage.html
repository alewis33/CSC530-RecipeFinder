Welcome to RecipeFinder

Let us help you find a recipe! Follow our Step By Step Tutorial below.



Step One - setting up your cabinet

  1. Select "My Cabinet" in the navigation above.
  2. Now select a cabinet you want to enter your ingredients in.
  3. Add your ingredients to your pantry, fridge, freezer, and spice rack.

Step Two - finding a recipe

  1. Now that you have added all of your ingredients, click the "Find me a Recipe!" button.
  2. Once you have found a recipe you like, save it to your cookbook!

Accessing your Recipes

Did you find a recipe you liked and saved it too your cookbook? Get that recipe again by clicking "My Cookbook" at the top of the page. From there you will find your saved recipe in one of your cookbooks!
