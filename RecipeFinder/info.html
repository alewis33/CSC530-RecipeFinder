About Recipe Finder

Recipe Finder is a web-based application that is used as a way for people to find and try new and exciting recipes. The user will enter their information as the first requirement, and then go through what the website has to offer. The cabinet portion is up first, where people can enter in ingredients, fridge and freezer items, and cabinet items into one of their cabinets. Once this is done, the user will then use the ‘Find me a Recipe’ button, and the button will then take them to google, where this search engine will enter in every item on the user’s list, and ONLY produce recipes that have the ingredients that the user has listed in their cabinet. Once they find a recipe they might enjoy making, the user can add this to their cookbook. Here, recipes can be stored for later user. The user will also have the option of having several different cookbooks and cabinets, all named what they wish to be named. There is also an option in the app where people can select between a variety of different dietary restrictions a user might have, which can be modified in the account settings section.

The reason that our team created the Recipe Finder web-based application is to help families, hard-workers, and people that need to cut back on expenses to look at their cabinet and think to themselves ‘Maybe I can make something from the leftovers I have in my kitchen!’ We all know that after shopping and cooking our own food for an entire week that there might be leftover items that we have not used or touched yet. We take this aspect and try to turn it into a positive instead of a negative. Instead of throwing your food away, go on our website and enter in all the leftover items you have. Then the website will try to find a recipe that fits all or just some of the food items you entered into the cabinet and show a bunch of recipes you can try. Hopefully, we can get people to stop wasting their food and money, and maybe find some recipes people would never have thought to try before!
